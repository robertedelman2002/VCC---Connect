📲 Install VCC Connect App

Access the Alachua Resource Directory with one tap from your home screen.

iPhone / iOS (Safari)

  1. Open this page in Safari browser.
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu.
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this page in the Chrome browser.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) menu icon.
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen."
  4. Follow the prompt to confirm installation.
